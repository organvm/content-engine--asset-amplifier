## 2. Client Scoring Rubric

### Scoring Criteria (1-5 scale per category)

**1. Content Investment (Weight: 25%)**
- 5 = Spends $50K+/month on content
- 4 = Spends $20K-$50K/month
- 3 = Spends $10K-$20K/month
- 2 = Spends $5K-$10K/month
- 1 = Spends <$5K/month

**2. Pain Level (Weight: 25%)**
- 5 = Explicitly stated pain (social media is a nightmare)
- 4 = Implied pain (inconsistent posting, low engagement)
- 3 = Aware of problem (knows they should post more)
- 2 = Mild concern (could be better)
- 1 = No apparent pain (happy with current state)

**3. Decision Authority (Weight: 20%)**
- 5 = Final decision maker (CEO/CMO)
- 4 = Strong influencer (Marketing Director)
- 3 = Committee decision (needs approval)
- 2 = Influencer only (no budget authority)
- 1 = No influence (wrong department)

**4. Timeline Urgency (Weight: 15%)**
- 5 = Needs solution in next 30 days
- 4 = Needs solution in next 60 days
- 3 = Needs solution in next 90 days
- 2 = Exploring options (no urgency)
- 1 = Just researching (no timeline)

**5. Relationship Strength (Weight: 15%)**
- 5 = Existing client (warm intro)
- 4 = Referred by client (warm intro)
- 3 = Met at event (warm-ish)
- 2 = Cold outreach (no connection)
- 1 = Complete stranger (no connection)

### Scoring Formula

**Total Score = (Content Investment × 0.25) + (Pain Level × 0.25) + (Decision Authority × 0.20) + (Timeline Urgency × 0.15) + (Relationship Strength × 0.15)**

**Score Interpretation:**
- 4.5-5.0 = **A+ Priority** (immediate outreach)
- 4.0-4.4 = **A Priority** (outreach this week)
- 3.5-3.9 = **B Priority** (outreach this month)
- 3.0-3.4 = **C Priority** (nurture relationship)
- < 3.0 = **Not a fit** (deprioritize)

## 5. Pilot Program Structure

**Activities:**
- Kickoff call (30 minutes)
- Brand identity setup (colors, tone, voice)
- Asset ingestion (1 hero asset)
- System configuration (platforms, scheduling)

- User trained on dashboard

**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ System configured correctly
- ✅ 30+ content units generated

- ✅ Content calendar approved

**Activities:**

**Deliverables:**

## 9. Risk Mitigation

### Risk #1: Pilot Client Churns

**Mitigation:**
- Set clear expectations (what system can/can't do)
- Provide excellent support (quick response times)
- Check in regularly (bi-weekly calls)
- Address issues immediately (don't let problems fester)

### Risk #2: Content Quality Is Poor

**Mitigation:**
- Test with real assets before pilot
- Optimize brand identity settings
- Provide manual override (approve/reject)
- Iterate based on feedback

### Risk #3: Technical Issues

**Mitigation:**
- Test end-to-end before pilot
- Have backup plan (manual content creation)
- Provide 24/7 support (for critical issues)
- Document bugs and fix quickly

### Risk #4: Low Engagement

**Mitigation:**
- Set realistic expectations (40% increase is target, not guarantee)
- Optimize content strategy (based on performance)
- A/B test different approaches
- Focus on quality over quantity
