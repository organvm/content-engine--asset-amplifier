## 1. Investor Deck Analysis (12 slides)

### Slide-by-Slide Review

**Slide 1 — Title**
- ✅ Strong: "One premium asset → continuous content → measurable yield"
- ⚠️ Missing: No subtitle explaining what it IS in plain English
- 💡 Recommendation: Add "The content yield engine for premium brands" as a one-liner

**Slide 2 — Problem**
- ✅ Strong stats: $15-50K per asset, 1-2 posts, $5-10K monthly labor
- ⚠️ Missing: No emotional hook or relatable scenario
- 💡 Recommendation: Add "Your client's $30K hero video sits unused after launch week"

**Slide 3 — Category**
- ✅ Excellent positioning: "Not a scheduling tool"
- ✅ Strong analogy: "Snowflake for data, Stripe for payments → content-to-revenue infrastructure"
- ⚠️ Risk: The analogy may be too technical for non-VC audiences
- 💡 Recommendation: Keep for VC, simplify for agency/product

**Slide 4 — Core Product**
- ✅ Clear flow: 1 asset → 10-15 fragments → 30-90 posts → performance report
- ✅ Three pillars: Extract, Generate, Optimize
- ⚠️ Missing: Visual mockup of the actual product
- 💡 Recommendation: Add screenshot or product demo GIF

**Slide 5 — System Architecture**
- ✅ Strong: 6-step pipeline with technical credibility
- ✅ Formula: `content = f(asset, identity, platform_state)` — signals defensibility
- ⚠️ Risk: Too technical for early-stage VCs
- 💡 Recommendation: Move to appendix or simplify to 3 steps

**Slide 6 — Differentiator (Natural Center)**
- ✅ Excellent: "Identity as a computable constraint"
- ✅ 5 dimensions: Thematic, Aesthetic, Tonal, Symbolic, Negative-space
- ⚠️ Missing: Concrete example of what this looks like in practice
- 💡 Recommendation: Add before/after comparison showing brand-consistent vs generic AI output

**Slide 7 — Output Multiplier**
- ✅ Strong stats: +40% engagement, -20 hrs weekly, 24/7 presence
- ✅ Clear before/after: 1 video → 1 post vs 1 video → 30-90 posts
- ⚠️ Missing: Source of these numbers (are they projections or actuals?)
- 💡 Recommendation: Add "Based on pilot data from [client]" or "Projected based on industry benchmarks"

**Slide 8 — Attribution**
- ✅ Strong: Full lineage from asset → fragment → content → performance → revenue
- ✅ Three capabilities: Time-decay scoring, platform normalization, multi-touch attribution
- ⚠️ Missing: Visual diagram of the attribution flow
- 💡 Recommendation: Add a simple flowchart showing how ROI is calculated

**Slide 9 — Audience Engine**
- ✅ Strong: "Content engine → audience formation engine"
- ✅ State transitions: Viewer → Engaged → Follower → Returning → Converted → Advocate
- ⚠️ Missing: How do you measure these transitions?
- 💡 Recommendation: Add metrics framework or link to analytics dashboard

**Slide 10 — Go-to-Market**
- ✅ Three buyers: Existing clients, Agencies, DTC brands
- ✅ Clear value prop for each
- ⚠️ Missing: Which segment are you targeting FIRST?
- 💡 Recommendation: Add "Phase 1: Lefler Design clients → Phase 2: Agency partners → Phase 3: DTC"

**Slide 11 — Roadmap**
- ✅ 5 phases: Content Loop → Attribution → Audience Engine → Experimentation → Multi-Tenant OS
- ✅ Clear progression
- ⚠️ Missing: Timeline (when will each phase ship?)
- 💡 Recommendation: Add Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 timeline or "Now / Next / Later"

**Slide 12 — Close**
- ✅ Strong: "Content → cost center becomes Content → compounding asset"
- ✅ Clear CTA: padavano.anthony@gmail.com

- 💡 Recommendation: Add "Seeking $500K seed" or "Looking for 3 pilot partners"

## 6. Mobile Optimization Issues

### Current State
- All decks use Reveal.js (desktop-first)
- Font sizes: 0.52em-0.78em (too small on mobile)
- Touch targets: 44px minimum ✅ (good)
- Layout: Single-column on mobile ✅ (good)

### Recommendations

**Immediate Fixes:**
1. Increase base font size from 20px to 24px
2. Increase stat values from 1.6em to 2em
3. Add more vertical spacing between elements
4. Simplify complex layouts (slides 5, 8 in investor deck)

1. Create mobile-specific slide variants
2. Add swipe gestures for navigation
