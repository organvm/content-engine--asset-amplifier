## The Transmutation Stages

1. **Nigredo (Black Stage / Ingestion)**: Raw, unorganized ideas in brainstorm text files are parsed by `cronus ingest` using regex heuristics into standardized Kerygma Profiles.
2. **Albedo (White Stage / Fragmentation)**: Raw video and image assets are broken down into granular fragments (clips, keyframes, audio hooks) by `@cronus/fragment-extraction`.
3. **Citrinitas (Yellow Stage / Synthesis)**: Resolved LLM providers generate platform-specific captions and hashtags. The Natural Center computes alignment scores and issues clarification inquiries for low-confidence identity dimensions.
4. **Rubedo (Red Stage / Staging & Release)**: The Surface Composer arranges media into triptychs and carousels, exports JSON manifests for instruments like NARCISSUS v0, and `apps/worker` dispatches published events while logging conversion funnel metrics.
